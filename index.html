Full transparency

Exactly what we log.
Nothing more.

You see everything. We hide nothing. This is what trust looks like.

What gets logged
Encrypted + hashed
Sender + recipients Who sent what to whom — structured metadata only
Subject line The email subject. Nothing beyond this.
Cryptographic timestamp ISO 8601, anchored permanently. Immutable.
SHA-256 hash of body Proves content existed — raw text is never stored or transmitted
What we never log
Never. Period.
Email body content Only a hash. We cannot read your emails. Structurally impossible.
Keystrokes or typing We are not a keylogger. Zero typing data is captured.
Browsing history Ledgey only activates on mail.google.com. Nowhere else.
Attachments or files File contents are never read, stored, or processed.
Any other apps or websites Gmail only. The extension has no access beyond this.
