Ingredient standards

What we include.
What we refuse.

01

Full disclosure, always

Every ingredient is named, sourced, and explained. No proprietary blends. If it is in the formula, it is on the label — with a reason.

02

No substances of concern

Triclosan, SLS, parabens, PFAS, synthetic dyes, and microplastics are absent from every product — well beyond regulatory minimums.

03

Tested for daily safety

Oral care is used twice daily, every day, for a lifetime. We formulate with cumulative exposure in mind — not single-use tolerances.

04

Sourced with intention

Raw materials are traceable to origin. Where botanical, wildcrafted or certified organic. Where mineral, independently assayed for purity.

``` Our purpose

We started NONTOXICCO
because we got tired
of being lied to.

For decades, the oral care industry has operated on a simple premise: make it cheap, make it foam, make it feel like it is working. The foaming is SLS. The whitening is abrasion. The freshness is synthetic fragrance. None of it is necessary. Most of it has never been tested for what happens when you use it every day for thirty years.

We are not scientists seeking a revolution. We are people who read labels, asked questions, and found that the answers were consistently unsatisfying. So we built the alternative we wanted for ourselves.

NONTOXICCO is not about fear. It is simply the belief that daily care should meet a daily standard — one that respects the intelligence of the person using it. If we cannot explain exactly what something does and why it belongs in the formula, it does not belong in the formula.

Three things we commit to

I.

Never use an ingredient we cannot fully disclose, explain, and stand behind — regardless of cost or convention.

II.

Hold every product to a standard of cumulative daily safety, not just regulatory compliance at point of sale.

III.

Never grow in a direction that requires compromising the above. Smaller and honest is better than scaled and inconsistent.

"We are not trying to replace your entire medicine cabinet. We are asking you to hold one small part of your daily ritual to a higher standard. That is enough."

The founders, NONTOXICCO
```
