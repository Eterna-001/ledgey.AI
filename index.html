How it works

Set it up once.
It runs forever.

1

Install in 60 seconds

Add the Chrome extension. Connect your Gmail. Toggle logging on. That is the entire setup. No configuration, no ongoing maintenance, no learning curve.

2

Everything logs automatically

Every email sent is timestamped cryptographically the moment you hit send. A SHA-256 hash of the content is generated locally — raw body never leaves your machine.

3

Ask The Librarian

Natural language queries over your entire company record. "Show me every decision around our term sheet." Instant, verified, cited. Always from the record.

4

Export proof when needed

One click to generate a legal-ready PDF with full cryptographic verification. Hand it to your lawyer. Send it to your board. Submit it to an auditor.
